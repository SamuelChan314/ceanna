Interests

Research Interests

My work sits at the boundary of analytical chemistry and energy science — developing methods that matter for both industry and environmental stewardship.

⚗️

Bitumen Extraction & Oil Sands Analysis

Developing and validating faster solvent-based extraction techniques for determining the oil, water, and solids content of Alberta oil sands ores — including dynamic accelerated solvent extraction (DASE).

🔬

Analytical Methods Development

Designing, optimising, and benchmarking analytical protocols for complex, heterogeneous sample matrices — with emphasis on speed, reproducibility, and practical applicability at scale.

🌱

Green & Sustainable Chemistry

Exploring solvent reduction, waste minimisation, and cleaner approaches to industrial chemistry — aligning laboratory methods with broader environmental and sustainability goals.

🧮

Physical & Environmental Chemistry

Understanding the thermodynamic and physicochemical properties of heavy hydrocarbon systems, including phase behaviour, wettability, and solvent–solid interactions in oil sands tailings.

Selected Projects

Analytical Chemistry · Research

Dynamic Accelerated Solvent Extraction of Oil Sands

Designed and optimised a dynamic accelerated solvent extraction (DASE) protocol to simultaneously determine oil, water, and solids content in Alberta oil sands — achieving complete extraction of 25 g samples in approximately 12 minutes. Benchmarked against industry-standard methods including Dean–Stark distillation and static ASE.
